##This is a working website ideas document for what the Dragon Ball Sparking Zero League central website will be like. This page will act as an information home page for DBSZL and navigation to all other public facing app sites and additional primary info (youtube, discord, etc.)

Website

Home Page:
- League banner at the top
- a brief description on what Sparking 0 League is, Com V Com, general outline of the season structure, Tenkaichi Bowl
- The current teams and their current lineups (Banners here eventually to make it look better)
- Discord link for people who want to sign on to a team
- Youtube link
- Light and dark mode button?

“Teams” Page
- A page describing each team, showing their ML, and again their current lineup, maybe with some more artistic elements added
- Each team could have a drop down with their ML, and any restriction to the team, and then a note attached to each character if they have any specific rules

“Archives Page”
-A page that holds the information from previous seasons, linking to past stats pages, the old website, maybe a shoutout to Squee for creating the league and to Mal for continuing it, links to the old Youtube channels, some other memorabilia?

“Community” Page
- A page outlining the various volunteer sections(Graphics/Art, Stats, Staff, Testers, Modders?, etc.) and the jobs they do to maintain the league
- A link to suggestions survey and testing page
- A Possible future Patreon link (someday maybe)

“Season Information” Page 
- A page to put the schedule for the season? This could also be put on the analyzer site since it’s essentially our stats sheet


Theming:
Obviously all the text in the same style
More colours than previous, but not obnoxious or blinding (medium blues, reds?, yellows? Idk I’m not good with this stuff)

Sparking 0 League banner at the top, a bar with the names of the 4 pages displayed underneath for people to access, then the homepage information displayed under that?
